A graphic depicting five example structural and navigational schemes generated as part of our co-design process, and applied to diverse chart types, including the following: (a) a multi-view scatter plot faceting different Barley varieties by their geographic location; (b) a choropleth map of the United States; (c) a stacked bar chart plotting the amount of a precipitation type by month of the year; (d) a line plot of German population by year, annotated according to East German or Nazi rule; (e) a multi-line chart plotting the stock price of Big Tech companies over time.
